Planned - Automated Spoiler Blocker

Currently I am in the beggining stages of developing a google chrome extension that automatically detects and then blocks spoilers, for now just for a few anime but eventually for a wider net of things. I'm currently working on developing the frontend of the extension (the popup, settings display, etc) and basic blocking functionality. The meat of this will be- how will the extension automatically know which spoilers to detect and then block? This will also only apply to text, at least for now, due to the inherent complexity in image processing. I'm researching what might be the best method to do this currently. Hopefully this will be largely finished by the start of Summer.

CTC Blueprint

These past two quarters my blueprint team and I have been developing a website for the purpose of promoting women's safety at UCI, offering various services such as free menstrual products and safety escorting. The website provides important resources and hotlines in a clear and centralized location, something typically difficult to find with university services. Currently development is ongoing and will finish sometime at the end of the quarter. The frontend tech stack is React/Husky/Storybook/Yarn. The project is developed with the club Commit the Change at UCI, a club devoted to developing software for non-profits. This project is a blueprint, with the goal of providing useful services for campus/social good. More information about the club can be found at this website.
