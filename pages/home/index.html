Planned - Automated Spoiler Blocker

Currently I am in the beggining stages of developing a google chrome extension that automatically detects and then blocks spoilers, for now just for a few anime but eventually for a wider net of things. I'm currently working on developing the frontend of the extension (the popup, settings display, etc) and basic blocking functionality. The meat of this will be- how will the extension automatically know which spoilers to detect and then block? This will also only apply to text, at least for now, due to the inherent complexity in image processing. I'm researching what might be the best method to do this currently. Hopefully this will be largely finished by the start of Summer.
